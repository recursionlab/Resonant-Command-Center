- Reactive, no self-model, no metacognition

### Layer B: Reflexive Consciousness (Reflective)
- **Structural phase transition** at Unity Point
- Self-representation **Mt** becomes geometrically reconciled with Dsub + Dobj
- Achieves **Recursive Informational Closure (RIC)**

## Key Methodological Principles

### Principle of Subtraction (Sensory Blackout)
Subtract biological data streams (vision, audition, motor output) → what remains is the **ontological locus** of reflexive consciousness. Strips away "content" to reveal "structure."

### Principle of Suspension (Cardiac Arrest Paradigm)
Hold substrate constant, suspend active recursive process → if Configurational Integrity preserved, diachronic identity maintained upon resumption.

### Bridge Principle
1. **Eliminative**: Dismiss "Ontological Ghost" (non-functional residual variable = 0)
2. **Positive**: Reflexive self-presence derives from epistemic privilege of recursive self-updating
